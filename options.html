GA Opt-Out (Per Site)

Google Analytics collect requests on these sites will be redirected to a local empty response. They still appear in DevTools Network with their full payload, but never reach Google.

Tips

  • Enter the registrable domain only (e.g. example.com). Subdomains are matched automatically.
  • Leading https:// and www. are stripped on save.
  • Matches any request to /g/collect whose query string contains tid=G-… — regardless of host.
  • This covers google-analytics.com (all regional subdomains) and server-side GTM relays on custom domains like gtm.example.com.
  • Universal Analytics (tid=UA-…) is deprecated and not matched.
